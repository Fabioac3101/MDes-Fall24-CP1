Remember antennas? Back in the days of radio transmitted television? You don't? Well I want to bring you that gift; the constant struggle I had as a child, but now for your zoom meetings. Show your engagement in the conversation with constant participation. Wanna be seen? JUST ADJUST THE ANTENNA? Whats an antenna, look it up; once you know what it is show it to the camera.

From my personal experience many analog experiences are slowly disappearing. This sketch is an exploration into capturing those experiences digitally. To get this to work I captured image model of variety of V shaped objects including hands. The model was giving false positives with my hand gestures and movements. The model was updated to focus just on 1 side views of the objects not 360. To improve the result the static screen would only disappear with an object confidence of 95% or more.
