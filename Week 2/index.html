Noblest of all dogs is the hot dog; it feeds the hand that bites it.

The Costco hot dog is popular and has never changed in price! It features an all-beef hot dog served in a soft bun for just $1.50, including a drink, making it a favorite among costconians.

The Colombian Hawaiian hot dog is a vibrant fusion, topped with pineapple chunks, mozzarella cheese, crushed potato chips, and drizzled with pink and pineapple sauces, offering a unique blend of sweet and savory flavors.

The Hot Texas Weiner is a deep-fried hot dog topped with mustard, onions, and a unique chili sauce, originating in Paterson, New Jersey, in the 1920s and becoming a regional favorite.
